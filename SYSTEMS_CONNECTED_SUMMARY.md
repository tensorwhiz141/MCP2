## 🔌 **API ENDPOINTS FOR DEVELOPERS:**

### **📄 PDF & Document APIs:**
```
POST /api/upload/pdf          - Upload PDF files
POST /api/upload/text         - Upload text content
POST /api/chat/pdf            - Chat with uploaded PDFs
POST /api/chat/document       - Chat with text documents
```
